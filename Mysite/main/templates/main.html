ABOUT MESHACK

My name is Meshack Kimwele and I am a Fullstack Software Developer. I currently live in Nairobi, Kenya. I'm an absolute geek and I love geeking out on all things Linux, Python and Flask.

I first fell in love with programming and Tech in general after highschool. I even enrolled for a bachelor's degree in Economics at University of Nairobi only to dropout in my first year. I realized that I was not into Ecnomics and technology was my thing. Technology has been taking up my life ever since! I started off with building apps in Java, my first programming languag which i was introduced to by a friend.

As of now, I am heavily focused on both front-end and back-end web development. My favorite development stacks at the moment are Python, Flask and Rest APIs.

Values I hold dearly are loyalty, reliability, authenticity and helping others soar higher.
